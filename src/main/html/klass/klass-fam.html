    Klass Family

    The Dutch side of him originates from his grandmother, Margaret Augusta Klass.

    Having spent much time bonding with her as he was growing up, he was invited into her world through her stories.

    James Sebastian Klass

    Cedric's great-great-grandfather was born in 1857. He grew up and was educated and married in Melaka.

    His pupilage was in the medical field that took him to Penang.

    From there in 1876 he made his way to Singapore where he established his family and career, starting as a dresser.

    In 1917, he was awarded the Imperial Service Medal by the King of England for his service at the General Hospital.
